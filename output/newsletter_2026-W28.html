Future Confidence Distillation in Large Language Models

arXiv · Score: 69/100

The paper studies how confidence signals evolve during LLM answer generation, showing that post‑solution confidence is better calibrated than pre‑solution confidence. 💡 Enables reliable confidence estimates early in the pipeline, reducing latency and cost for retrieval, tool use, and adaptive computation in production systems.
